About

I am Zixiong Wang (王子雄). I am a Ph.D. candidate supervised by Prof. Beibei Wang. I received my Master's degree from the Interdisciplinary Research Center (IRC) of Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Shiqing Xin.

My research primarily focuses on computer graphics, where I strive to bridge the gap between the digital and real world. Specifically, I work on rendering, geometry learning, and 3D vision. I welcome any opportunity for discussion and collaboration. Please feel free to reach out anytime!
